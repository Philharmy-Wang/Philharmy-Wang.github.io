## Research Focus

My work centers on computer vision methods for non-human primate behavior understanding. The goal is to build reliable perception systems that can support long-term monitoring, individual-level analysis, and behavior interpretation in realistic animal observation environments.

## Directions

<div class="card-grid">
  <div class="info-card"><strong>Computer Vision</strong><br>Detection, recognition, estimation, and representation learning for biological and remote sensing scenarios.</div>
